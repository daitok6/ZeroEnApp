# Template: Step 4 — Breakup

**Day:** +18 · **Target length:** ~80 JP chars · **Honor the breakup — do not soften it into another pitch**

---

## Purpose

Close cleanly. This is the last touch. A breakup email that actually closes often gets the highest reply rate of the sequence because it removes pressure. Do not use this as an excuse for one more pitch.

---

## Structure reference

```
[Subject: 「一度クローズします」]

[名前]さん、

最後のご連絡です。
今は不要そうなのでこちらはクローズします。

またいつかタイミングが合えば、zeroen.devからご連絡ください。

[name]
```

---

## Variants

**If they opened every email but never replied:**
```
何度かお送りしましたが、今はお忙しい時期なのかもしれません。
一度クローズしますね。またタイミングが来たらぜひ。
[name]
```

**If 6 months pass and timing might be different:**
- You may re-open a new sequence from step 1 with a fresh angle.
- Do NOT send a "just checking in" reopen. Start with a new, specific observation.

---

## Do NOT

- Add another offer or CTA
- Say "last chance" or create artificial urgency
- Write more than 4 lines
- Ask why they didn't reply

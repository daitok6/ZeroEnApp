## 4. X / Twitter JP Signal

Hashtags surveyed: `#LP制作`, `#ホームページ制作`, `#個人事業主`, `#副業`.

- `#LP制作` shows daily posts but low virality (typical post: 0-20 likes, 0-5 RT). Audience is selling-to-selling freelancers. ([note.com/hashtag/lp](https://note.com/hashtag/lp) shows 5,331 posts — same pool).
- `#個人事業主` is the strongest buyer-side tag: 100+ posts/day with occasional 500+ like hits (tax, tools, routine questions). High-intent audience for the ZeroEn ICP.
- `#副業` is flooded with affiliate noise — low quality-to-volume ratio.

**Engagement sweet spot:** 1k-50k follower accounts have highest eng rate per [Japan Influencer research](https://collabstr.com/top-influencers/twitter/in/japan). Target micro-influencer LP freelancers (10-30k follower WebDesigners) for partnership/referrals.

Influencers worth engaging:
- JP WebDesigners tagged on note with UTAGE/STUDIO expertise (referral partnerships, not followers)
- `#個人事業主` micro-influencers offering tax/tools content — audience overlap

- **Strongest single signal:** Coconala category 500 (ホームページ作成) — **12,115 active listings, ~50,000 cumulative deliveries claimed, median flat price ¥150k**. The market is proven and enormous; ZeroEn's angle is the recurring-revenue reframe no one else offers.

# Market Saturation — JP Solopreneur LP Segments

**Owner:** mktg-strategy · **Date:** 2026-04-14 · **Decision deadline:** 2026-04-18 (Phase 0 gate)

---

## Why this doc exists

ZeroEn has 90 days to ¥300k MRR. 45 paying clients at ¥5–10k/mo. The single biggest lever is **which sub-segment we aim the first 8 weeks of content, Coconala listing copy, and cold outbound at.** This doc ranks 7 JP solopreneur segments on 5 dimensions and names the top 1–2 to target.

**Market frame:**
- JP freelance population: **1,303万 (13.03M), ¥20.3 trillion economy, 2024** — [Lancers 2024 フリーランス実態調査](https://www.lancers.co.jp/news/pr/24055/)
- 22.8% of JP labor force is freelance (US is 36% → still growing). [freelance-start.com](https://freelance-start.com/articles/326)
- 個人事業主 ホームページ 保有率: **only 18.3% use HP/SNS for customer acquisition** — 株式会社ビーブレイクシステムズ 2021 — via [Wepage](https://wepage.com/blog/article/63/index.html). **81.7% gap = TAM**.
- Freelance HP pricing on Coconala for an LP: **¥50k–¥100k upfront** (source: [優良WEB](https://yuryoweb.com/coconala-hp/)); full HP ¥150k–¥300k.
- Incumbent DIY: ペライチ 50万+ users, skews 40s–50s SMB/solopreneur ([ペライチ プレス](https://peraichi.com/news/press-20200908)); STUDIO ¥1,190–¥9,980/mo, designer-heavy.

**ZeroEn's asymmetry:** free build + ¥5k/mo subscription. The entire JP market charges ¥50k–¥500k upfront. No one competes on our axis.

## Scoring rubric

| Dimension | Scale |
|---|---|
| Segment size in JP | S (<100k) / M (100k–500k) / L (500k–2M) / XL (2M+) |
| Saturation of LP solutions | 🟢 underserved · 🟡 light · 🟠 heavy · 🔴 saturated |
| WTP for ¥5–10k/mo | 1 (won't pay) → 5 (pays today for worse) |
| Fit to ZeroEn offer | 1 (wrong shape) → 5 (mirror image) |
| Urgency (actively searching now) | 1 (passive) → 5 (searching weekly) |

Total = sum (5–25). Saturation inverts (🟢=5, 🔴=1) for ranking.

## Segment notes

### A. Solo consultants — score 20 (runner-up)
- Size: 経営・IT・法務・税務 consultants inside the 297万 自由業系フリーワーカー + 500万 自営業系独立ワーカー pool ([Lancers 2024](https://www.lancers.co.jp/news/pr/24055/)).
- WTP: bills clients ¥50k–¥300k/mo themselves. ¥5k/mo is rounding error.
- Saturation 🟠: many agencies court them. They don't want another agency — they want a site that makes them look legit without burning a week.
- Risk: scope creep. Fix with locked-scope contract (already in change-catalogue.md).

### B. Coaches / therapists — **score 22 · WINNER**
- Size: estimated ~1,000 active coaches ICF-registered + multiples that number in self-identified コーチ/セラピスト/カウンセラー (no hard census). Market: ¥30B in 2019, 6× growth in 4 years ([Ecole](https://www.e-cole.co.jp/coaching-marketsize/)).
- **Pain is documented and screaming:** "起業初期段階で計画的でないスタートをして顧客がゼロの状態" — [rankup-stage.com](https://rankup-stage.com/coaching-web-attracting-customers/). Direct quote: ホームページやランディングページの作成とセールスライティングが集客に直結することが課題.
- WTP 4: they buy ¥198k–¥500k coach-training courses. ¥5k/mo is a latte. Existing paid LP niche: women-instructor ペライチ seminars ([hitonowa](https://hitonowa.hp.peraichi.com/1day-lplogic/)).
- Urgency 5: their entire sales funnel is the LP. No LP = no clients. They know it.
- Fit 5: product = LP + monthly edits as they iterate messaging/niche. Exactly our SKU.
- Saturation 🟡: the 集客 coaches who sell to coaches are everywhere, but **free-build + hosting as-a-service doesn't exist in JP**.
- Coconala validation: LP/HP listings crowded but targeted at generic SMB — no coach-specific provider dominates.

### C. Course creators / 先生業 — score 19
- Real demand (ストアカ lists dozens of LP courses for instructors — [street-academy.com](https://www.street-academy.com/zenkoku/lp)).
- But ¥5k/mo is high for course-launch-and-done buyers. Churn risk after launch.

### D. Side-hustle creators — score 13
- XL volume, near-zero WTP. Most want ¥0 tools (ペライチ free tier, Note). Skip.

### E. Local service (salons, clinics) — score 13
- XL volume, but their acquisition is MEO / Hot Pepper Beauty / Instagram, not LP. Wrong product. ([AI-BOUZ 2026](https://ai-bouz.com/salon-marketing-2026/))

### F. Solo SaaS founders — score 15
- Right mindset, small market in JP, prefer building themselves. Route to `/startups` equity pitch not SaaS.

### G. 士業 個人事務所 — score 19 (strong but slow)
- 82,276 registered 税理士 as of Dec 2025 ([agaroot.jp](https://www.agaroot.jp/zeirishi/column/tax-accountants/)). Trust-heavy vertical.
- Very willing to pay ¥5–10k/mo for "always-on" presence.
- Slow sales cycle, long objections list ("why free? what's the catch?"), conservative buyers. **Phase 2 expansion target, not Phase 1.**

## Recommendation

### Primary target (Weeks 1–8): **Coaches, therapists, counselors (コーチ・セラピスト・カウンセラー)**

Why this one: highest total score (22), screaming pain, pre-educated on "LP = clients" equation, category spends ¥100k–¥500k on training but their web presence is a ペライチ template. Existing coach-to-coach sellers have proven WTP — ZeroEn just undercuts upfront cost to zero.

**Copy angle:** 「あなたの言葉が、クライアントを連れてくる。LP構築は無料。月¥5,000で運用まで任せてください。」

**Where to fish:** Coconala category [216 ホームページ作成](https://coconala.com/categories/216), Note topic コーチング/起業, X JP hashtags #コーチング #個人起業, ストアカ instructor directories, women-in-coaching Facebook groups.

### Secondary (Weeks 4+): **Solo consultants (A) as spillover audience**

Same copy, swap proof point. Already inside our current WebMori orbit — zero extra research cost.

### Explicit no's (Phase 1)

- 副業 crowd — won't pay
- Local service — wrong product (MEO beats LP)
- 士業 — too slow for 90-day push; queue for Phase 2

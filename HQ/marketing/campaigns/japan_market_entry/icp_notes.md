# ICP: Japan Market Entry

**Segment ID:** `japan_market_entry`
**Priority:** HIGH (secondary ICP #1)

## Profile

Foreign company entering Japan — needs a bilingual marketing site or product for the Japan launch. Budget approved, wants a trusted partner, not a procurement process. GM or Country Manager is the decision-maker, not a procurement team.

## Signals

- "Japan launch" / "expanding to Japan" in company news or LinkedIn posts
- Foreign HQ with no Japan office yet (pre-launch)
- Job posting for Japan GM / Japan Country Manager
- Product is EN-only; need JP localization + legal compliance

## Pain Points

- "We need this live before the Japan launch date"
- "Our HQ dev team doesn't understand Japanese legal requirements"
- "We tried translation agencies — they don't build products"
- "The Tokyo office doesn't exist yet; we need a trusted remote partner"

## Channels

- Cold email via LinkedIn (Japan GM, Country Manager, VP APAC titles)
- Expand into Japan media (e.g., JETRO partner events, EO Tokyo)
- Referrals from foreign-founder network in Tokyo

## Key Message

"Launching in Japan? You need bilingual from day one — not translated after the fact. We handle EN/JA routing, tokushoho compliance, and JP-style billing. Fixed price, shipped in weeks."

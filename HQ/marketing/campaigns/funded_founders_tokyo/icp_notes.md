# ICP: Funded Founders Tokyo

**Segment ID:** `funded_founders_tokyo`
**Priority:** HIGH (primary ICP)

## Profile

Foreign founder in Tokyo who just closed a seed or pre-seed round. English-first, bilingual need, urgent timeline, cash on hand. Has already learned that Japanese agencies quote ¥8M and 4 months. Understands the value of a fast, bilingual, production-quality build.

## Signals

- Recently announced funding (LinkedIn, Crunchbase, Startup Digest Japan)
- Founder is non-Japanese (or Japan-based but English-first)
- Hiring or posting about "Japan launch" / "bilingual product"
- Active in Tokyo foreign founder communities (FFTJ, Tokyo Tech meetups)

## Pain Points

- "I can't find a technical cofounder"
- "Japanese agencies quote ¥8M and 4 months"
- "Offshore shops don't understand bilingual UX"
- "I need this live before my runway narrows"

## Channels

# ICP: Funded Japanese Startups (English-Facing)

**Segment ID:** `japanese_startups_global`
**Priority:** MEDIUM (secondary ICP #4)

## Profile

Pre-IPO or post-seed Japanese startup that needs an English-facing product for global fundraising, hiring, or market expansion. Technically literate leadership but no bandwidth to manage a dev project. The English product is a fundraising tool, not the core product.

## Signals

- Product is Japanese-only; company is raising from global/US/EU investors
- Job postings for English-speaking roles
- Founder or exec posting about "global expansion" or "overseas fundraising"
- Product published on AppStore/PlayStore JP only

## Pain Points

- "Our product is Japanese-only and investors keep asking for an English version"
- "We have a dev team but they're all focused on the JP product"
- "We need an EN landing page / investor demo in 3 weeks before our roadshow"

## Channels

- Cold email via LinkedIn (founder, CEO, CTO at post-seed JP startups)
- IVS / B Dash Camp / Slush Tokyo community adjacent
- X (JP tech founder community)

## Key Message

"Investors want to see your product in English. We build the EN-facing surface — fixed price, shipped before your roadshow."

## Content Themes

- "The minimal EN-facing product for a JP startup's global fundraise"
- "Bilingual SaaS architecture: building JP and EN from one codebase"

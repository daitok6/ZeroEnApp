# ICP: Bilingual Recruiting & Staffing

**Segment ID:** `bilingual_recruiting`
**Priority:** MEDIUM (secondary ICP #2)

## Profile

Bilingual recruiting and staffing agencies in Tokyo. Candidate acquisition through web presence. Revenue model depends on inbound — a strong bilingual site directly drives pipeline. Budget is clear; agency owner understands ROI from web.

## Signals

- EN/JA bilingual job board or recruiting site that looks dated
- Agency serves foreign companies hiring in Japan, or Japanese companies hiring abroad
- Active LinkedIn presence posting bilingual job listings

## Pain Points

- "Our site is Japanese-only and we're losing foreign client inquiries"
- "We've been meaning to rebuild for two years — never get to it"
- "We tried a web agency; they didn't understand bilingual UX or recruiting flows"

## Channels

- Cold email via LinkedIn (agency owner, director titles)
- JREC-IN and Daijob community adjacent outreach
- Direct referral from foreign founder contacts who use recruiting firms

## Key Message

"Your bilingual recruiting site is your pipeline. We build EN/JA sites that convert — fixed price, shipped in under a month."

## Content Themes

- "Why bilingual recruiting agencies lose leads with JP-only websites"
- "The bilingual web stack for Japan-based staffing firms"

# ICP: Foreign-Owned SMBs in Tokyo

**Segment ID:** `foreign_owned_smb`
**Priority:** MEDIUM (secondary ICP #3)

## Profile

Foreign-owned SMBs in Tokyo operating on a Western revenue model where the website directly drives sales. Owner is often the decision-maker. Has budget (or can see the ROI case clearly). Online presence is a bottleneck to growth.

**Important qualifier:** Excludes flower shops, cafes, restaurants (unless foreign-owned chain), solo coaches, and any business where the website is purely informational and doesn't drive revenue.

## Signals

- Foreign-owned business in Tokyo with an outdated or JP-only site
- Service-based or product-based with clear inbound revenue potential
- Owner active on LinkedIn or visible in Tokyo expat business community

## Pain Points

- "Our site is JP-only and we lose English-speaking clients"
- "The Japanese agency quoted us ¥5M and 6 months for a rebuild"
- "We need EN/JA and it needs to actually convert, not just look nice"

## Channels

- Cold email (owner-direct, Tokyo expat business community)
- EO Tokyo / AmCham / BritCham / EuroCham Tokyo
- Direct referrals from foreign founder network

## Key Message

"Your website should be generating leads in both languages. We build bilingual sites that convert — fixed price, in weeks."

## Content Themes

- "Why EN/JA isn't just translation: how bilingual web presence drives revenue"
- "The ¥880k bilingual site vs. the ¥5M agency quote"

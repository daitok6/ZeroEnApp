## The Problem

Most startup ideas die before they're built.

Not because the idea is bad. Not because the founder gives up. Because they can't find a technical co-founder who believes in their idea enough to work for equity.

The alternatives are bad:

→ Hire a dev agency. $50k+ for a bloated codebase you don't own.

→ Learn to code. 18 months later, your market moved.

→ Find a co-founder. Good luck convincing a senior engineer to leave their job for your unvalidated idea.

## What We're Doing Differently

ZeroEn builds your MVP for free.

No upfront cost. We take 10% equity. We become your technical co-founder.

If your idea is worth building, we build it. If it takes off, we share in the upside.

That's the whole model.

## Why This Works

AI has fundamentally changed what a solo developer can ship. With the right tooling, one engineer can build what used to require a team of five.

We've built that tooling. ZeroEn is a system — not just a service — for building and operating startup MVPs at scale.

Next.js. Supabase. Vercel. A repeatable stack. A quality system. An operating model.

We build fast. We build well. We build free.

## What We Look For

Not every idea qualifies. We score every application on four dimensions:

→ **Viability** — Is this a real problem with real demand?

→ **Commitment** — Are you serious about building this?

→ **Feasibility** — Can we build a meaningful MVP in scope?

→ **Market** — Is there a path to revenue?

Score 15 out of 20 or higher, and we talk.

## The Bet

We're betting on founders. We're betting that the best ideas, built well and launched fast, create value.

If you have the idea and the commitment, we have the execution.

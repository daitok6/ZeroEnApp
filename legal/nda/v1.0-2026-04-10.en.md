# Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement

**Version:** v1.0-2026-04-10
**Governing Law:** Ontario, Canada (see Section 10 of the Partnership Agreement)

Both ZeroEn and the client agree to keep each other's confidential information private.

**Confidential information includes:**
- Application data and business plans
- Technical specifications and system architecture
- Financial projections and revenue data
- User research and market analysis
- Any other information shared during the application, onboarding, or build process

**Obligations:**
Neither party will share, copy, or use the other's confidential information without written consent.

**Standard exclusions apply:**
Information that is publicly available, independently developed, or legally required to be disclosed is not covered by this agreement.

**Data deletion:**
Any applicant — accepted or rejected — may request deletion of all their submitted data at any time. Rejected application data is automatically deleted within 30 days of the rejection decision.

**Duration:**
This confidentiality obligation survives the termination of any agreement between ZeroEn and the client. It remains in effect for 3 years following termination for general business information, and in perpetuity for personal data subject to applicable privacy law.

**Electronic acceptance:**
By typing your name and checking the acceptance box during the application process, you confirm you have read and agree to this Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement.

# ZeroEn — Build in Public Strategy

**Date:** 2026-04-09
**Status:** Approved

---

## 1. Core Strategy

The engine is: **every client build is a content event.**

Before, during, and after shipping an MVP, the build is documented. Each client generates content across all platforms without extra creative work. The operator log runs in parallel — weekly revenue updates, deal outcomes, milestone posts — as the connective tissue between builds.

### Content Hierarchy

| Priority | Type | Description |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Anchor** | Client build case study — one per client, multi-platform |
| 2 | **Pulse** | Weekly operator update — numbers, deals, decisions |
| 3 | **Reactive** | Replies, threads, hot takes as they happen (X only) |

### 90-Day Arc

| Month | Focus |
|---|---|
| Month 1 | Establish EN presence, post origin story, document first build in real time, lock in content rhythm |
| Month 2 | Publish first case study, launch EN newsletter, establish Instagram rhythm, layer in JP accounts |
| Month 3 | Video drops, referral loop starts, inbound leads from content |

### 90-Day Success Metrics

- **Clients:** 3-5 inbound leads directly from content
- **Audience:** 1,000+ followers across platforms
- **Revenue:** First $1K/mo from platform fees

---

## 2. Audience

| Segment | Platform | What they want to see |
|---|---|---|
| Founders | LinkedIn, X, Instagram | Proof it works, real client builds, outcomes |
| Builders / indie hackers | X | The process, raw numbers, honest failures |
| Investors / ecosystem | X, newsletter | Track record, deal structure, growth metrics |

All segments are served simultaneously — anchor content (builds) attracts founders, pulse content (operator updates) attracts builders and investors.

---

## 3. Platform Strategy

### Fully Faceless — No Camera, No Face, Ever

ZeroEn is the face of the brand. The operator never appears. All video content is screen recordings with text overlays. No voiceover required (optional AI voice). No profile photos with the operator's face — ZeroEn logo is the avatar on all accounts.

---

### X (Twitter) — Primary Feed

- **Avatar:** ZeroEn logo
- **Cadence:** 1 post/day minimum, threads 2x/week
- **Content mix:** 50% build updates · 30% operator transparency · 20% hot takes
- **Growth tactic:** Reply to founders complaining about dev costs — that's the customer in the wild
- **Accounts:** `@ZeroEn` (EN) · `@ZeroEn_JP` (JP, launched Month 2)

---

### Newsletter / Blog — Owned Audience

- **Author:** ZeroEn (not the operator's name)
- **Cadence:** Every Monday
- **Structure:** `[ZeroEn #N] — {one honest sentence about the week}`
- **Content:** 1 client build deep dive OR operator update + what shipped + what's next
- **Blog:** Cross-posted publicly for SEO
- **Lists:** EN list (Month 1) · JP list (Month 2)

---

### Instagram — Visual Proof

- **Cadence:** 3x/week
- **Format:** Reels primary (60-90s) · Carousels for case studies
- **Content:** Screen recordings, terminal output, product demos, revenue dashboards, text overlays + music
- **No:** Face, voiceover, camera footage of any kind
- **Accounts:** `@ZeroEn` (EN) · `@ZeroEn_JP` (JP, launched Month 2)
- **Repost strategy:** Same visual content posted to both accounts — text overlays translated

---

### Video (YouTube) — Faceless Build Demos

- **Format:** Screen recordings only, no webcam ever
- **Narration:** Text overlays (optional AI voice for audio)
- **Cadence:** One video per client build
- **Repurpose pipeline:** Long YouTube → 60s Reel → X clip
- **Channel:** One EN channel (JP subtitles added, no separate channel needed)

---

## 4. Bilingual Approach

**Strategy: Separate accounts, EN first.**

Launch EN in Month 1. Lock in the content rhythm and templates. Launch JP in Month 2 using translated EN content.

| Account | Language | Launch |
|---|---|---|
| `@ZeroEn` | English | Month 1 |
| `@ZeroEn_JP` | Japanese | Month 2 |
| EN Newsletter | English | Month 1 |
| JP Newsletter | Japanese | Month 2 |

**Translation workflow:** AI generates first draft → operator polishes (fast, given fluency). Visual Reels require only translated text overlays — no re-recording.

## 6. Per-Client Content Event

Every new client triggers this content sequence:

| Step | Content | Platform | Timing |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Kickoff post — "Just signed client #N. Here's what we're building and why." | X thread EN → JP | Day of signing |
| 2 | Build updates — 2-3 posts as it progresses | X + Instagram clips | During build |
| 3 | Ship post — "MVP is live. Built in X weeks. Here's what it looks like." | X thread + Reel | Launch day |
| 4 | Case study — full write-up | Newsletter + blog + YouTube | Week after launch |

---

## 8. What Not to Do

- No face, no camera, no voiceover — ever
- Don't launch EN and JP simultaneously — get EN rhythm locked first
- Don't create content separately for each platform — repurpose from one anchor piece
- Don't post vanity metrics — only real numbers (revenue, clients, weeks to ship)
- Don't over-produce — raw and real outperforms polished and corporate for this brand
